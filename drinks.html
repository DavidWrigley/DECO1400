Two Birds Golden Ale "Featured Winner"

Two Birds Golden Ale is brewed using ale, wheat and Vienna malts. It is bittered with New Zealand Southern Cross hops while Motueka and Australian Summer hops are used for aroma. Motueka contributes melon and citrus aromas and flavours, while Summer contributes spice and apricot notes.

Cost: $9

Monster Mash IIPA

500ml Bottle. Red honey/amber colour with the a faint brown tint. Pours a frothy, off white head, dissipating to a thin but excellently laced head. Strong citrus like aromas of grapefruit and orange, with notes of pine, nectarines and spices. Initial taste of a bitter caramel malt flavour with hints of peach and pine, very smooth. Finish is a piney/citrus flavour and very bitter.

Cost: $12
