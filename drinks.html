Two Birds Golden Ale

Two Birds Golden Ale is brewed using ale, wheat and Vienna malts. It is bittered with New Zealand Southern Cross hops while Motueka and Australian Summer hops are used for aroma. Motueka contributes melon and citrus aromas and flavours, while Summer contributes spice and apricot notes.

Cost: $9

Monster Mash IIPA

Red honey/amber colour with the a faint brown tint. Pours a frothy, off white head, dissipating to a thin but excellently laced head. Strong citrus like aromas of grapefruit and orange, with notes of pine, nectarines and spices. Initial taste of a bitter caramel malt flavour with hints of peach and pine, very smooth. Finish is a piney/citrus flavour and very bitter.

Cost: $12

Willie Smiths Apple Cider

Beer-like, and a subtle maple flavor. with an Intense fruitiness and no detectable alcohol taste, it is simular to a Belgian beer with a slight apple taste. with a great amount of bubbles, it lends itself to those who feel adventurouse and like to try new things

Cost: $7

Holgate Road Trip IPS

A super-charged but dangerously sessionable All-American hop-bomb, this golden hued IPA is loaded up with multiple additions of Chinook, Centennial and Citra hops from the Pacific Northwest of the USA, resulting in an explosion of piney, citrus and grapefruit flavour and aroma. Holgate's tribute to the best brew from the USA!

Cost: $11

Brasserie Dupont Saison Dupont

Saison Dupont is a classic Belgian farmhouse ale. This is a beautifully balanced, complex beer that has a refreshing fruitiness and long, dry finish. It is bottled unfiltered so it may be cloudy or have a slight sediment but this is normal and perfectly natural.

Cost: $13

Van Steenberge Celis White

Celis White is a white wheat top-fermented beer with a touch of fruit and herbs. If served cold, it gets a blurred white appearance and a suprising light taste. This delicious white beer got all the time it needs to develop.

Cost: $7

Bacchus Tamira Porter

This is it, crafted to perfection for over 20 years. Crack it open, and the first sip tastes as fresh as ever. The unmistakeable deep-dark colour. The crisp hint of roasted barley, the fresh breeze of hops. The refreshing bite. The bittersweet reward. Pure beauty

Cost: $10

Schlenkerla Eiche Doppelbock

You will be blown away by this beer's rich savory flavors and silky smooth body. recommend for general craft beer fans as a fantastic representation of the smoked beer style. This beer tastes like wood-fired sugar bacon.

Cost: $10

Bacchus Hibiscus Saison

Blood red in colour. Champagne like, rich berry aromas, combine with intense flavours of cranberry, black currant and cherry, giving way to a mango nectar. Silky in texture but finishes dry and refreshingly tart.

Cost: $10

Nail Imperial Choc Stout

Nail Imperial Choc Stout pours super dark with a rich lasting heading. The flavor is intense - a balance of dark malts, fig, chocolate and mocha, finishing rich and satisfying.

Cost: $10

Rogue XS Imperial Stout

Rogue XS Russian Imperial Stout is rich in texture with a nice white head. The body is broad, soft and creamy, very robust and full
